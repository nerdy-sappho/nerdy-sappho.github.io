Wishes for the new year (2025)

 02/01/2025

So the new year is already here...wow. It's time to make new plans!

But first I'd like to recap the previous year.


Good things from 2024:

  • you could say I have my academic shit put together;
  • I remade the whole website and I love how it looks now;
  • I managed to set some boundaries on how I spend my time;
  • I drew quite a lot!! Though I stopped posting, maybe when ai rules will be more clear I'll start again;
  • it was pretty fun overall :3

2025 resolutions:

  • remember the word resolution by memory and how to spell togheder (*together);
  • stop over-analyzing and overthinking my own thoughts;
  • get a driver licence;
  • find a way to express my emotions in an heathy way (writing, drawing, idk);
  • read more, watch more shows, spend free time in a way I genuinely enjoy (rather than scrolling or staring at the ceiling);
  • find a way to study my favorite things more regularly (programming, art stuff, etc.).

Half year goals:

These are more pratical but set in an easier to manage period of time than a whole year.

  • keep up with the school work! I'm doing good already so I can't give up now;
  • check and update something on this website at least once a month;
  • practice art basics more (something like once a week would be great);
  • same goes for coding!!
  • write down thoughts and ideas, don't let them just float in my head like super-elaborate clouds;
  • feed my creativity again, make new things, make characters, make worlds, basically stop just doing art and start creating again. I feel like it's been hard to do this in the last moths and I don't wanna lose it completely.

So this is all! I'm feeling a bit awkard writing this to be honest, I generally just make lists in my notebook without adding much else.


Thanks for reading and have a splendid 2025!
